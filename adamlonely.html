Lonely Adam

In the 3 to 4 oclock frame in the outer circle, we see the scene from Genesis 2 where Adam feels alone. God creates Adam from the earth and places him in the Garden of Eden with the beasts and birds. God presents the creatures to Adam and asks Adam to give the creatures names. At some point, Adam realizes there is not another creature like him. The caption in this scene, Adam n(on) inveniebatur simili sibi (Adam found nobody similar to himself) is a paraphrase of Gen. 2:20
